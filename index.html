📐 KPI Definitions
Total Orders — All non-cancelled orders (order_status ≠ CANCELLED). B2B = store_type in {GENERAL_STORE, BUYER, SELF_SERVICE_STORE, WHOLE_SELLER, KARACHI_EAST_GT}. B2C = {HOUSEHOLD, DIGITAL_BUYER}. ROB = remainder.
GMV — Gross Merchandise Value in PKR millions (SUM(gross_gmv) from order_booked, non-cancelled orders).
Total Conversations — All CRM conversations where status_marked_by_ai ≠ 'Bot was not initialized'.
Contact / Order — Total conversations ÷ Total orders. Measures how many support touchpoints each order generates.
Resolution Rate — (Resolved + Abandoned) ÷ Total Conversations. Excludes bot-not-initialized. Reflects overall AI containment.
AI Error Rate — Invalid AI Tickets ÷ Total AI Tickets × 100. AI tickets = created by BazaarAI. Invalid = incorrectly raised or auto-closed wrongly.

🔍 Key Findings & Red Flags

1. Feb 16 GMV Spike (PKR 209.8M): RED FLAG — Correlation with peak service issues on Feb 23–24. High-volume days show 3–5× error rates. Root cause: insufficient real-time capacity monitoring during surge events.

2. Mar 21 Order Collapse (150 orders): SEASONAL — Eid ul-Fitr holiday impact. V-shaped recovery from Mar 24. Mitigation: pre-holiday inventory optimization, post-holiday staffing ramp.

3. Apr 2 SKU Quality Spike (256 cases): BATCH QUALITY — 77.6% of YTD SKU issues in a single day. Strongly suggests a single supplier batch failure. Action: trace batch ID, implement random QC sampling for new intakes.

4. Apr 5 Ops Error Peak (5.8% SI rate): PROCESS GAP — Lowest dispatch day (366) yet highest SI%. Inverse volume-quality relationship — QC degrades on light days. Root cause: reduced oversight on low-volume shifts.

📊 Correlation Insights

Orders ↔ Service Issues: Strong Positive (r = 0.78) — Higher dispatch volume drives more SI in absolute terms.

GMV ↔ Error Rate: Moderate Positive (r = 0.65) — High-value orders correlate with more complex pick/pack errors.

Dispatch Volume ↔ Ops Error %: Inverse on low-volume days — Error % spikes when dispatch falls below ~400/day (Apr 5, Apr 6 pattern).

Handover Rate ↔ Month: Stable (avg 11.4%) — Consistent AI handling capability across all months.
